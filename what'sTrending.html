What is baggy clothing

Baggy clothes are loose-fitting. Sometimes kids wear their pants so baggy that their legs look like toothpicks in a lunch bag. It's easy to hide in baggy clothes, and they're usually really comfy. The adjective baggy describes oversized or roomy clothes.

Are ripped jeans in Style in 2022?

Frayed hems had their moment in the sun but are now on their way out. Replacing them are the loose-fitting ripped jeans the fashion crowd is obsessed with. They're sure to be a staple in 2021 as an of-the-moment way to bring a laid-back quality to any look.
